About

Teachers' Thrift Cooperative Society provides a range of financial products and services namely savings, loans and financial consultation to its members

Brief History

Teachers' Thrift Cooperative Society executive board was formed in 2020. The first ever meeting was held on April, 2020 with four members of the executive board. Teachers' Thrift Cooperative Society was registered in 2021 with 25 members (4 executive members & 21 ordinary members)
